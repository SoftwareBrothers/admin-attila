An effective way to manage your business data?

The out-of-the-box version of AdminJS is pretty powerful, but its in-depth customizability is where it really shines. With a basic knowledge of React and Node.js, you can change nearly every behaviour of your admin panel. With AdminJS you can Create, Read, Update and Delete all of your resources, no matter where they come from. Thanks to the tight integration with your ORM/ODM, AdminJS picks up all the validation rules, data types and relationships.
